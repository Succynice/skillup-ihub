Welcome to Skillup iHub

Skillup Imo was founded by the Imo state government by His Excellency, Dist.Sen Hope Uzordinma the Executive Governor of Imo state. The project was created to help the imo youths develop ,and enhance their tech driven talents in this 21st century.

The Hon.Commissioner, Dr Chimezie Amadi Commissioner for Digital Economy and eGovernment has made the program so far a huge success, through his inter lecture and innovative thinking ability to develop great minds.
